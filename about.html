About Naima's Frozen Yogurt Shop

Our Story

Naima's Frozen Yogurt Shop was born from a simple dream: to create a place where people can enjoy delicious, healthy frozen treats that bring smiles to their faces. Founded by Naima Aisyah, our shop combines passion for quality ingredients with a love for creative flavors.

What started as a small family recipe has grown into a beloved local destination. Every day, we craft our frozen yogurt fresh in small batches to ensure the creamiest texture and the most vibrant flavors.

Our Mission

We believe that dessert should be both delicious and nourishing. That's why we use real fruits, premium dairy, and natural sweeteners. Our yogurt contains live and active cultures, making it a treat you can feel good about enjoying.

Why Choose Us?
